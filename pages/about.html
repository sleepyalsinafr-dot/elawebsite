About

Kiwiville is the perfect town and vacation for anyone who wants to get away from their imperfect town. Kiwiville uses points called "Kiwipoints" which allow you to upgrade your house. You can gain kiwipoints by working and helping maintain food, resources, and sometimes by helping out. Sensors in the ground automatically award you with kiwipoints for doing things and punish you by removing them for breaking the rules. The government is a council that was voted for and make descisions and the peoples vote for them. This are called public projects which can include: buildings, furniture, outdoor furniture.
